## Key Insights for ArryBarry

1. **Price sweet spot:** £9.99–£13.75 for 30ml is the dominant range for organic serums with credible review scores. Below £5 risks perceived quality issues.
2. **Certification matters:** Weleda's "100% Certified Natural" and Dr Organic's certified organic claims are highlighted prominently. Buyers are searching for this.
3. **Review volume threshold:** Products with <100 reviews struggle for visibility. ClaRose's dominance at 985 reviews shows that even budget organic serums can win on Amazon with enough social proof.
4. **Discount culture:** UpCircle (34% off RRP), Weleda (25% off), Dr Organic (18% off) — heavy discounting is normalised in this category. RRP anchoring is important.
5. **Key ingredients driving conversion:** Hyaluronic acid, vitamin C, peptides, retinol, and rose oil are the most-featured organic serum ingredients.
