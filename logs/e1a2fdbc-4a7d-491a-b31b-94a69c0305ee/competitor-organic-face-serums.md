## Strategic Implications for ArryBarry

- **Price gap at mid-market (£25–£35):** UpCircle (£21.77) → Evolve (£32) shows an underserved band. A strong organic serum at ~£28–£30 with clean formulation could sit competitively.
- **Review volume matters:** Neal's Yard and UpCircle have the strongest review scores. Any ArryBarry listing should prioritise review generation strategy from day one.
- **Sensitive skin is under-served in organic category:** Only Pai explicitly targets this — but at £49+, there's room for an accessible sensitive-skin-certified organic serum at a lower price.
- **Sustainability storytelling is table stakes:** Every competitor leads with organic/vegan/cruelty-free. Differentiation must go deeper — provenance, upcycled ingredients, B-Corp, or clinical efficacy.
- **Ingredient transparency is a purchase driver:** Consumers in this category read ingredient lists. Clear INCI labelling and hero ingredient spotlighting is essential.
