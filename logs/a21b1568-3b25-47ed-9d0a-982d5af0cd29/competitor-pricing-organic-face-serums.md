## Strategic Observations for ArryBarry

1. **Price gap at £20–£28** — Only Bee Cosmetics occupies this space. An organic serum in this range with strong ingredients and review volume could compete directly.
2. **Bee Cosmetics dominates social proof** — 1,437 reviews at 4.9★ is a high bar. Differentiation via ingredient story or sustainability angle needed.
3. **Sustainability/upcycled angle** — UpCircle owns this positioning at low price. Premium sustainable serum remains open territory.
4. **Certified organic** is table stakes — all top competitors are certified or claim high organic %. Certification should be prominently featured.
5. **Hyaluronic + Vitamin C combo** is the most competitive formula pairing — consider differentiation via less common actives (peptides, bakuchiol, adaptogens).
6. **Amazon presence** — Pai, UpCircle, and Green People all sell via Amazon. Listing on Amazon will be necessary for visibility in this category.
