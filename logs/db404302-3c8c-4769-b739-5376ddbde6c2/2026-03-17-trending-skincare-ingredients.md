# Top 5 Trending Skincare Ingredients UK 2026

*Research brief — 17 March 2026*

---

## 1. Exosomes
Tiny cellular messengers that carry proteins and lipids to support skin regeneration and repair. Searches up 81% in recent months. Found primarily in serums; improve texture, tone, and firmness. Leading the "regenerative skincare" wave.

## 2. Ectoin
Natural amino acid from extremophile microorganisms. Searches up 86% recently. Locks in moisture, strengthens the skin barrier, and protects against environmental stressors. Key for sensitivity and barrier-repair positioning.

## 3. Beta-Glucan
Complex polysaccharide from oats, yeast, and fungi. Searches up 181% year-on-year (avg. 2,900/month). Boosts hydration, supports skin's natural repair processes, and soothes inflammation. Strong alternative to hyaluronic acid.

## 4. Amino Acids
Biochemical building blocks for regenerative aesthetics — described as "the ingredients to polynucleotides' recipe." Set to dominate professional and consumer skincare following the polynucleotide boom of 2025.

## 5. Spicules (Marine Sponge-Derived)
Micro-needle-like particles from marine sponges, trending out of South Korea. Called "microneedling in a bottle" on social media. Emerging breakthrough for at-home skin resurfacing and renewal.

---

## Market Context
- UK mass skincare sales up **21%** (Kantar, Jan 2026); premium up **7%**
- 1.2 million more UK shoppers buying skincare vs prior year
- Overarching theme: **longevity, barrier strength, and science-backed regeneration**
- Fading: over-exfoliation, skin cycling, and shock-value actives
